CONCEPT

Denver Broncos' fans are able to scan these collectible souvenir cups and Bud Light can to unlock augmented reality content. This includes access to game highlights, cheerleaders, and more!

MY ROLE

I worked directly with the Broncos' video production crew to procure the right shots of the cheerleader and the mascot. I designed all of the cup and can experiences, and created most of the visual effects.

UNIQUE CHALLENGE

The Denver Broncos wanted a unique experience to come from each of their collectible cups. In addition, the Bud Light can was to have its contents updated with highlights every two weeks.

To meet the demands of such a rigorous release schedule, I worked closely with a local and international team. I structured the outsourced work to be modularly implemented in order to minimize workflow bottlenecking.
