Justice Served

General Mills and DC Comics teamed up to produce a series of Justice League cereal boxes.

The Project:

The Project:

Create an augmented reality campaign from these boxes.

My Objective:

Bring the comicbook art to life.

Objective:

Objective:

Bring the comicbook art to life.

Unique Challenge:

Superman is displayed front and center in this artwork. The biggest challenge in animating him was giving believable motion to his cape.

At first I tried puppet-warping the existing cape, but I soon realized this solution wouldn't work. The bright sunlight reflecting from the top of the cape and the light passing through the red material made any shortcut animation tricks too unrealistic.

The cape moves freely in the wind, and it would take too long to draw each frame by hand.
