UNIQUE CHALLENGE

The animation of each superhero came with its own set of challenges. Superman's cape was certainly one of the most complex elements. In order to create a more believable motion for the cape in action, I utilized a physics simulation.

First, I created a 3D model of the cape. Then, I turned the rigid model into an nCloth object in Autodesk Maya. After customizing the stiffness across the fabric and setting the wind speed and turbulence parameters, I exported the cape animation. Finally, I matched the lighting in the 3D environment to what's seen in the art, and I applied custom shaders onto the fabric to give it the comic-book appearance.
